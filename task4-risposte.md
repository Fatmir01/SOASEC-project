To align with the objectives of the Gender Equality Strategy regarding women's entrepreneurship, organizations and stakeholders can take specific actions:

1. **Access to Financial Assistance**:
   - Develop and promote financial products specifically designed for women entrepreneurs, such as low-interest loans, grants, or funding competitions targeted at women-led startups.

2. **Entrepreneurship Training Programs**:
   - Create tailored entrepreneurship education and training modules focusing on key business skills, including marketing, finance, and leadership, specifically for women.

3. **Networking and Mentorship Initiatives**:
   - Establish networking platforms or mentorship programs that connect aspiring women entrepreneurs with successful female business leaders to provide guidance, advice, and support.

4. **Awareness Campaigns**:
   - Promote awareness campaigns to highlight the contributions of women entrepreneurs to the economy and society, showcasing success stories and initiatives that support gender equality in business.

5. **Monitoring and Reporting**:
   - Implement measures to track the participation of women in entrepreneurship programs and the effectiveness of initiatives aimed at supporting women entrepreneurs, making necessary adjustments based on findings.
